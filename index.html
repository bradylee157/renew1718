What Is Renew?

Renew is not just another Bible study! In this curriculum, you will:

  • Move from personal renewal to missional living
  • Dive into content and application
  • Share life in intergenerational relationships
  • Hear Sunday sermons that help you thrive during the week
  • Engage in one-on-one discipling relationships to go even deeper
  • Facilitate church-wide shift toward missional living

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Renew?

Renew is EP’s ongoing Gospel-renewal experience, designed to develop the people of EP as Jesus-loving, mission-pursuing disciples. We invite everyone to join a Renew Group as we work together to begin a movement of Gospel-centered renewal in the greater Annapolis area.

I’m not in a group. Can I still join?

Yes! We are starting book two the week of January 7, so it is a great time to jump into a Renew Group. Sign up anytime and join one of these small, intentional communities committed to discipling one another and modeling God’s family to our city.

What if I missed a sermon? Where can I listen?

We have all of our sermons online. Visit our SoundCloud page or subscribe to our iTunes podcast. You can also download our app (App Store/Google Play) and tap the Sunday tab or visit our website.

What if I’m already in a group? Do I need to register again?

No. You are all set and will continue to meet with your group just as you did for the first book. We also encourage you to continue (or start) your one-on-one meetings to walk with each other in learning and application. And do please contact us if you need anything as you go.

Evangelical Presbyterian Church

Over the past five decades, EP Church has helped thousands of people in Annapolis and the surrounding area find meaning, purpose, and belonging. With Jesus as Lord, our desire is to be a group of believers renewed by the Gospel who in turn are seeking the renewal of our city and those who live here. The Gospel has changed who we are, and we hope to live out that good news so that others may fall in love with our Savior.
